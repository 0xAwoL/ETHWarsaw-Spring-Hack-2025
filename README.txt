# FOOM Club AI Automation: Bringing Genius Minds Back to Life

## 🚀 The Challenge

FOOM Club is pioneering a bold vision: **daily token drops**, each backed by the **reincarnation of a historical genius**.  
But to engage audiences and fuel excitement, every drop needs **compelling, high-quality content—fast**.

### ⚠️ The Problem

Creating engaging, consistent content for each persona takes **too much time**.

Each video must feature a **historical genius speaking**—telling their story or encouraging users to buy their token.

> **Manual content creation is unsustainable** when scaling to daily drops.

---

## 💡 The Solution

We built an **AI automation model** that integrates multiple AI tools to **fully automate content creation**.

In just **1 minute**, FOOM Club can generate a **high-quality video** of a reincarnated genius—**no manual effort required**.

### 🔹 How It Works

FOOM Club’s founders simply:

- ✅ Enter a historical figure’s full name  
  _e.g., Albert Einstein, Nikola Tesla, Leonardo da Vinci_
- ✅ Type what the persona should say after reincarnation
- ✅ Choose the video format:  
  - `9:16 (vertical)` for Reels, TikTok, and social media  
  - `16:9 (horizontal)` for YouTube and widescreen platforms
- ✅ Select the character’s position in the frame

> The AI model automatically generates a **lifelike video**, making the reincarnated persona speak as if alive today—**in under 1 minute**.
